Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra — Best Android Phone in UAE

Titanium build, 200MP camera, Galaxy AI and the S Pen. Samsung's most powerful phone at a significant discount on Amazon.ae.

Galaxy AI — The Headline Feature

The S24 Ultra is Samsung's first phone built around AI. Circle to Search lets you draw around anything on screen to instantly search it — useful when you spot a product in a video and want to find it on Amazon. Live Translate handles real-time phone call translation, which is genuinely useful in the UAE's multilingual environment.

Chat Assist rewrites messages and translates them on-the-fly across WhatsApp, Telegram, and other messaging apps. Note Assist summarizes meeting recordings and generates action items.

Camera System

The 200MP main sensor captures extraordinary detail. In well-lit UAE outdoor scenes — think desert landscapes, Burj Khalifa at sunset, or marina shots — the results rival dedicated cameras. The 5× optical zoom (up from 3× on the S23 Ultra) makes it excellent for event photography and capturing distant subjects.

  • 200MP wide (f/1.7) with OIS
  • 12MP ultrawide (f/2.2)
  • 50MP 5× telephoto periscope
  • 10MP 3× telephoto
  • 12MP front camera

Night mode is improved, and ProVideo shoots 8K at 30fps. For social media content creators in the UAE, this phone can replace a separate camera for most situations.

Display & Performance

The 6.8-inch QHD+ Dynamic AMOLED panel hits 2,600 nits peak brightness — the brightest phone screen you can buy, and crucial for outdoor readability in UAE's intense sunlight. 120Hz refresh rate keeps everything fluid.

The Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 handles everything from heavy multitasking to Genshin Impact at max settings. With 12GB RAM, apps stay in memory longer, so switching between WhatsApp, email, and a browser with 20 tabs is seamless.

Build & S Pen

The titanium frame is a noticeable upgrade — it's more resistant to drops and scratches than the aluminum of previous models. The flat display edges make the S Pen more natural to use for note-taking and document signing.

The S Pen is built in (no separate case needed) and responds at 2.8ms latency. For professionals who sign documents, annotate PDFs, or sketch ideas, it remains a unique advantage over every other phone.

Battery & Charging

  • 5,000mAh battery — comfortably lasts a full day
  • 45W wired charging (0–65% in 30 minutes)
  • 15W wireless charging
  • 4.5W reverse wireless charging for Galaxy Buds or Watch
