What is Stream Deck Neo?

The Stream Deck Neo is Elgato's latest entry-level macro keyboard featuring 8 customizable LCD keys and 2 touch points. Unlike traditional keyboards, each key displays a custom icon and can trigger complex macros, app launchers, or media controls with a single press.

It's designed for both streamers who need scene switching and everyday professionals who want to automate repetitive tasks in apps like Excel, Word, Zoom, and Spotify.

Key Features

  • 8 LCD Keys — Each key has its own display for custom icons
  • 2 Touch Points — Quick access to pages and actions
  • Drag-n-Drop Setup — No coding required
  • Multi-App Integration — Works with OBS, Zoom, Teams, Spotify, and more
  • Mac & PC Compatible — Plug and play via USB-C
