Which robot vacuum is best for UAE apartments?

For UAE apartments with tile and marble floors, robot vacuums with strong mopping capability are ideal. The Xiaomi X20 Pro and Roborock series handle both vacuuming and mopping well on hard floors and deal with the sand/dust common in UAE homes.
