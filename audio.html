Which wireless earbuds are best for UAE weather?

For UAE's hot climate, look for earbuds with IPX4 or higher sweat resistance. The Apple AirPods Pro, Sony WF-1000XM5, and Samsung Galaxy Buds are all rated for moisture resistance and perform well in warm conditions.
